## Agile development:
- Also called, Agile Methodology.
    - Its a flexible, iterative approach to project management that emphasizes collaboration, continuous delivery and adaptability.
    - It values people, customer feedback and working solutions over ridig processes and documentation.
    - Teams tailor agile practices to their needs to blend frameworkds like Scrum and Kanban for optimal results.
    - Experiments with agile practices and regularly review their effectiveness to foster continuous improvement and team collaboration.
